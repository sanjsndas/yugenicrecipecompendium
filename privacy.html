🔒 Privacy & Data Stewardship

At Yugenic Recipe Compendium, we honor your trust as much as we honor ancestral recipes. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any personal information you share with us. We are fully GDPR & CCPA compliant and committed to culinary-level transparency.

🔥 How We Use Your Information & Legal Grounds

Under GDPR/CCPA, we rely on the following lawful bases: contractual necessity (e.g., answering a direct inquiry), legitimate interests (improving our content), and consent (newsletter subscriptions). We process your data exclusively for:

  • 📬 Responding to your contact form, recipe submissions, or support requests (usually within 24 hours).
  • 📧 Sending occasional Yugenic newsletters if you opted-in via the contact page or workshop forms; every email includes an unsubscribe link.
  • 📊 Statistical analysis & site improvements (aggregated, anonymized data).
  • 🧑‍🍳 Featuring user-submitted recipes with proper credit and necessary permission.
  • ⚖️ Legal compliance, fraud prevention, and enforcing our Terms.
✨ Retention: Contact form inquiries are retained for 24 months unless a deletion request is made. Recipe submissions may be kept indefinitely as part of the compendium archive, but attribution can be anonymized upon request.

🤝 Sharing & Third-Party Processors

Yugenic never sells your data. However, we rely on trusted service providers to run our digital kitchen:

  • Web Hosting & Security: Secure cloud infrastructure (data stored in US/EU regions)
  • Email & Communication: Email delivery services for support replies and newsletters (e.g., SendGrid / Mailchimp – GDPR compliant).
  • Analytics: Privacy-friendly aggregated analytics (no cross-site tracking).

All processors sign data protection agreements. We do not share data with advertisers or brokers. If we ever introduce interactive features (user accounts), we will update this policy and obtain explicit consent.

🍪 Cookies & Similar Technologies

To enhance your experience — remembering your preferred language, saving dark/light mode (future), and understand site traffic — we use essential and analytical cookies. You can manage preferences via browser settings.

🍯 session_id (essential) 🌾 preference_theme (functional) 📊 ga_analytics (anonymized) 🍋 yugenic_consent (cookie choice)

By continuing to use the Yugenic Compendium, you agree to the use of cookies as described. You may disable non-essential cookies via our cookie banner (appears on first visit) or browser settings. Third-party embeds (e.g., recipe video players) may set their own cookies; we don't control them.

🛡️ Your Privacy Rights (GDPR, CCPA & Global)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be forgotten): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to Object/Opt-out of marketing: Unsubscribe at any time via link.
  • Do Not Sell My Personal Information (CCPA): We do not sell data, but you may still submit a request.

To exercise your rights, email privacy@yugenicrecipes.com or call +1 (800) 594-GOLD. We verify your identity before processing requests (to protect your data). We respond within 30 days. No fees for standard requests.

📍 European residents: You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. California residents: see our “Notice at Collection” in the full policy annex.

🔒 Security & International Transfers

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS), access controls, and regular security audits to protect your data. In the unlikely event of a personal data breach, we will notify affected users and supervisory authorities as required by law (within 72 hours where mandatory). Our servers are located in the US and EU; by using our site, you consent to data transfer, understanding we maintain Privacy Shield / SCCs protections.

👧 Children's Privacy

Our compendium is intended for general audiences (13+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
